Experience with Aframe

The goal of this assignemnt was to gain experience in creating a 3D scene. In groups, we worked to produce a 3D scene from a chosen image. As shown by the images on the left and right, my group decided to create our 3D scene based on an image of Spider-Man. The original image depicts two Spider-Man subjects, a brick wall, and a police car. These were the major characteristics of the image that we wanted to capture in the constructed image.

My experience with the Aframe interface was not as complex as I anticipated. However, working with HTML was proven to be very complex when it comes to perfectly placing items in their desired position. While the items in the constructed scene are not exact replicas of the original image, we chose to blend items that were most similar.
